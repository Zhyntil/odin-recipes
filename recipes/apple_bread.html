Apple Bread

Description

A simple sweet apple bread recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
  • 3 cups apples (peeled and chopped)
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Steps

    Preheat oven to 300F(150C)

    2 loaf pans (9x5) with cooking spray

  1. Mix flour, baking soda, salt, apples and walnuts in a large bowl.
  2. Mix oil, sugar, eggs and cinnamon in a medium bowl.
  3. Combine the two mixures
  4. Evenly divide mixture between the two loaf pans
  5. Bake in oven for 90 mins or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool in the pans for 15 minutes, then let cool completely on rack
